Learn Algebra

Status: Developed and Released

Build a mobile application that provides students an accessible tutoring program that allows them to study on their own time and schedule.

Created, with the rest of the team at ExEquals, an Android application with three features: learn, practice, and quicknotes. The learn section contained an abridged algebra textbook, the practice section would randomly generate practice problems with solutions and explanations, and the quicknotes section contained a sparknotes summary of the most important topics in algebra.
